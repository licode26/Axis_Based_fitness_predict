Nutrition and Protein for Healthy Weight Gain

High-Protein Foods

  • Lean Meats: Chicken, turkey, lean beef, pork
  • Fish: Salmon, tuna, and fatty fish
  • Eggs: Full of protein and healthy fats
  • Dairy Products: Whole milk, yogurt, cheese
  • Plant-Based Proteins: Beans, lentils, quinoa, tofu
  • Nuts and Seeds: Almonds, walnuts, chia, flaxseeds

Carbohydrate-Rich Foods

  • Whole Grains: Brown rice, oats, quinoa, barley
  • Starchy Vegetables: Sweet potatoes, potatoes, corn
  • Legumes: Lentils, beans, peas
  • Fruits: Bananas, apples, berries, avocados

Healthy Fats

  • Avocados: Rich in healthy fats
  • Olive Oil and Coconut Oil: For cooking
  • Nuts and Nut Butters: Peanut butter, almond butter
  • Seeds: Sunflower seeds, chia, flaxseeds

Calorie-Dense Foods

  • Smoothies: With fruits, nuts, yogurt, protein powder
  • Granola and Trail Mix: High in calories and nutrients
  • Full-Fat Dairy: Milk, yogurt, cheese
  • Dark Chocolate: Rich in healthy fats and antioxidants

Protein Supplements

  • Protein Shakes: Add protein powder to smoothies
  • Weight Gain Shakes: For a balanced mix of nutrients
