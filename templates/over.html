Nutrition for Overweight

Focus on reducing calorie intake while ensuring adequate nutrition.

Key Nutrients:

  • Protein: Helps with satiety; examples include chicken, tofu, beans.
  • Fiber: Aids in digestion and fullness; examples include oats, broccoli, apples.
  • Healthy Fats: Promote satiety; examples include avocados, nuts, olive oil.
  • Low-Glycemic Carbs: Provide sustained energy; examples include quinoa, sweet potatoes, lentils.
